About me

I am Vishnu Chipade, a Robotics Researcher. I am currently working as a Senior Researcher at the Secure Systems Research Center in Technology Innovation Institute, Abu Dhab, UAE. I have a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA. My research involves bringing Robotics and Artificial Intelligence together to create intelligent robotic systems that coordinate together, collaborate with and assist humans to perform wide range of tasks safely, securely and resiliently.
